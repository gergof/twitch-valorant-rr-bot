Live Chat Rank Tracking

Show your Valorant RR changes on stream.

This application connects your Twitch channel with a tracked Riot ID, watches for live streams, checks your Valorant RR while you are streaming, and posts updates to chat when a new match is detected.

Access the app
Automatic live detection The bot reacts to Twitch live status and also runs periodic reconciliation to recover from missed events.
Chat commands Viewers can ask for the current rank with !rr or !rank.
Free to use The public deployment is available at twitchrr.systest.eu.

How It Works

After you sign in with Twitch and save your Riot ID, the bot listens for your live status, opens a tracked stream session, polls your RR during the stream, and stores match updates.

Setup

  1. Open the dashboard.
  2. Sign in with Twitch.
  3. Enable the bot.
  4. Enter your Riot ID as Name#TAG.

Chat Output

When a new match is detected, chat receives the RR change, current rank, current RR, and a running total for the stream session.

Use the dashboard to configure your channel and review tracked streams and matches. The application itself is available at /dashboard.
